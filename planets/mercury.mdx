# Mercury (Budha) in Vedic Astrology

Mercury, known as Budha in Sanskrit, represents intellect, communication, commerce, and learning. It is the planet of intelligence and governs our ability to think, speak, and process information.

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: Budha
- **Nature**: Benefic (when with benefics), Malefic (when with malefics)
- **Element**: Earth
- **Gender**: Eunuch/Neutral
- **Own Signs**: Gemini (Mithuna) and Virgo (Kanya)
- **Exaltation**: Virgo (15 degrees)
- **Debilitation**: Pisces (15 degrees)
- **Moolatrikona**: Virgo (16-20 degrees)

## Significations

Mercury represents:

- **Intellect and Intelligence**: Mental abilities, analytical skills, learning
- **Communication**: Speech, writing, expression, languages
- **Commerce and Trade**: Business, trade, accounting, mathematics
- **Education**: Learning, teaching, students, knowledge
- **Siblings**: Brothers and sisters, cousins
- **Nervous System**: Brain, nerves, mental health
- **Skin**: Complexion, skin health
- **Speech**: Voice, eloquence, oratory skills
- **Mathematics**: Calculation, logic, reasoning

## House Significations

Mercury is particularly significant for:

- **2nd House**: Speech, family, wealth through communication
- **3rd House**: Communication, siblings, courage, writing
- **4th House**: Education, mother, property
- **5th House**: Education, children, creativity, intelligence
- **10th House**: Career in communication, teaching, business
- **11th House**: Gains through intellect and communication

## Strength Factors

Mercury is strong when:

- Placed in Gemini or Virgo (own signs)
- In Virgo (exaltation)
- In angular houses (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th)
- In 5th house (intelligence bhava)
- Conjoined with benefics (Jupiter, Venus)
- In own Nakshatra
- During daytime birth

## Weakness Factors

Mercury is weak when:

- Placed in Pisces (debilitation)
- In 6th, 8th, or 12th houses
- Combust (too close to Sun)
- Conjoined with malefics (Saturn, Mars)
- During nighttime birth

## Mercury's Dual Nature

Mercury takes on the nature of planets it associates with:

- **With Benefics**: Becomes benefic, promotes learning and communication
- **With Malefics**: Becomes malefic, causes speech problems, mental stress
- **With Sun**: Becomes combust, loses independence

## Dasha Period

- **Vimshottari Dasha**: 17 years
- **Effects**: Periods of learning, communication, business activities, education, relationship with siblings, intellectual pursuits

## Remedies

- Worship on Wednesdays
- Chanting Budha mantras
- Wearing emerald (Panna)
- Fasting on Wednesdays
- Donating green items, books, or educational materials
- Reading and learning activities
