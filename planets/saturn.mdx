# Saturn (Shani) in Vedic Astrology

Saturn, known as Shani in Sanskrit, represents discipline, karma, delays, hard work, and spirituality. It is the planet of justice and teaches us through challenges and restrictions.

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: Shani, Shanaishchara
- **Nature**: Malefic (but can be benefic when well-placed)
- **Element**: Air
- **Gender**: Eunuch/Neutral
- **Own Signs**: Capricorn (Makara) and Aquarius (Kumbha)
- **Exaltation**: Libra (20 degrees)
- **Debilitation**: Aries (20 degrees)
- **Moolatrikona**: Aquarius (0-20 degrees)

## Significations

Saturn represents:

- **Karma and Justice**: Past actions, consequences, justice
- **Discipline and Hard Work**: Perseverance, dedication, labor
- **Delays and Obstacles**: Slowness, delays, restrictions
- **Longevity**: Old age, longevity, chronic diseases
- **Servants**: Service, servitude, labor class
- **Legs**: Lower limbs, bones, joints
- **Teeth**: Dental health, teeth problems
- **Hair**: Hair loss, baldness
- **Poverty**: Financial difficulties, scarcity
- **Spirituality**: Detachment, renunciation, spiritual practices

## House Significations

Saturn is particularly significant for:

- **3rd House**: Siblings, courage, hard work, communication
- **6th House**: Enemies, diseases, service, debts
- **8th House**: Longevity, obstacles, transformation
- **10th House**: Career, karma, profession, authority
- **11th House**: Gains through hard work, friends, aspirations
- **12th House**: Expenses, foreign lands, spirituality, liberation

## Strength Factors

Saturn is strong when:

- Placed in Capricorn or Aquarius (own signs)
- In Libra (exaltation)
- In angular houses (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th)
- In 10th house (karma bhava)
- In 11th house (gains bhava)
- In own Nakshatra
- During nighttime birth

## Weakness Factors

Saturn is weak when:

- Placed in Aries (debilitation)
- In 6th, 8th, or 12th houses (though 8th can be strong)
- Combust (too close to Sun)
- During daytime birth
- In enemy's sign

## Sade Sati (7.5 Years)

Saturn's transit over natal Moon creates Sade Sati:

- **Duration**: 7.5 years (2.5 years before, 2.5 years on, 2.5 years after Moon)
- **Effects**: Challenges, transformations, spiritual growth
- **Remedies**: Chanting, charity, spiritual practices

## Dasha Period

- **Vimshottari Dasha**: 19 years
- **Effects**: Periods of hard work, delays, obstacles, discipline, karma, service, spiritual growth, transformation

## Remedies

- Worship on Saturdays
- Chanting Shani mantras
- Wearing blue sapphire (Neelam) or black onyx
- Fasting on Saturdays
- Donating black items, iron, or to the needy
- Serving the elderly and performing charity
