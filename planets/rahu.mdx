# Rahu in Vedic Astrology

Rahu, the North Node of the Moon, represents desires, material pursuits, illusions, and worldly ambitions. It is a shadow planet (Chaya Graha) that creates obsessions and drives material success.

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: Rahu
- **Nature**: Malefic
- **Element**: Air
- **Gender**: Male
- **Own Signs**: None (but strong in Aquarius, Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius)
- **Exaltation**: Gemini (20 degrees)
- **Debilitation**: Sagittarius (20 degrees)
- **Moolatrikona**: Gemini (0-20 degrees)

## Significations

Rahu represents:

- **Desires and Ambitions**: Material pursuits, worldly ambitions
- **Illusions and Deception**: Maya, delusion, confusion
- **Foreign Lands**: Foreign countries, immigration, travel abroad
- **Technology**: Modern technology, computers, internet
- **Addictions**: Substance abuse, obsessions, vices
- **Outcasts**: Social outcasts, unconventional people
- **Snakes**: Serpents, reptiles, poison
- **Grandfather**: Maternal grandfather
- **Smoking**: Tobacco, intoxicants
- **Unconventional**: Breaking traditions, modern thinking

## House Significations

Rahu is particularly significant for:

- **3rd House**: Courage, communication, siblings, short journeys
- **6th House**: Enemies, diseases, service, competition
- **9th House**: Foreign lands, higher learning, spirituality (material)
- **10th House**: Career, fame, public recognition, authority
- **11th House**: Gains, income, friends, aspirations, material success

## Strength Factors

Rahu is strong when:

- Placed in Gemini (exaltation)
- In angular houses (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th)
- In 10th house (karma bhava)
- In 11th house (gains bhava)
- In own Nakshatra
- Conjoined with benefics (Jupiter, Venus)

## Weakness Factors

Rahu is weak when:

- Placed in Sagittarius (debilitation)
- In 6th, 8th, or 12th houses
- Conjoined with malefics (Saturn, Mars)
- In enemy's sign

## Rahu's Effects

Rahu creates:

- **Material Success**: Sudden gains, wealth, fame
- **Obsessions**: Strong desires and attachments
- **Illusions**: Confusion, deception, misunderstandings
- **Foreign Influence**: Connection with foreign lands and people
- **Unconventional Path**: Breaking traditional norms

## Dasha Period

- **Vimshottari Dasha**: 18 years
- **Effects**: Periods of material pursuits, foreign connections, technology, obsessions, sudden gains, unconventional experiences

## Remedies

- Worship on Saturdays or during Rahu Kaal
- Chanting Rahu mantras
- Wearing gomed (Hessonite) or agate
- Fasting on Saturdays
- Donating blue or black items, or to outcasts
- Avoiding intoxicants and addictions
