# Ketu in Vedic Astrology

Ketu, the South Node of the Moon, represents spirituality, detachment, liberation, and past-life karma. It is a shadow planet (Chaya Graha) that promotes renunciation and spiritual enlightenment.

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: Ketu
- **Nature**: Malefic
- **Element**: Earth
- **Gender**: Eunuch/Neutral
- **Own Signs**: None (but strong in Scorpio, Pisces, Virgo, Sagittarius)
- **Exaltation**: Sagittarius (20 degrees)
- **Debilitation**: Gemini (20 degrees)
- **Moolatrikona**: Sagittarius (0-20 degrees)

## Significations

Ketu represents:

- **Spirituality and Liberation**: Moksha, enlightenment, spiritual awakening
- **Detachment**: Renunciation, letting go, non-attachment
- **Past Life Karma**: Previous life experiences, karmic debts
- **Mysticism**: Occult sciences, astrology, tantra
- **Isolation**: Solitude, seclusion, isolation
- **Wounds and Cuts**: Injuries, surgical scars, accidents
- **Grandmother**: Maternal grandmother
- **Poison**: Venom, toxins, antidotes
- **Research**: Deep investigation, research, analysis
- **Intuition**: Psychic abilities, sixth sense, intuition

## House Significations

Ketu is particularly significant for:

- **4th House**: Mother, home, property, spirituality
- **8th House**: Longevity, transformation, occult, research
- **9th House**: Spirituality, higher learning, dharma, father
- **12th House**: Liberation, foreign lands, expenses, spirituality
- **6th House**: Enemies, diseases, service, spiritual service

## Strength Factors

Ketu is strong when:

- Placed in Sagittarius (exaltation)
- In angular houses (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th)
- In 8th house (moksha bhava)
- In 12th house (moksha bhava)
- In own Nakshatra
- Conjoined with benefics (Jupiter, Venus)

## Weakness Factors

Ketu is weak when:

- Placed in Gemini (debilitation)
- In 6th, 8th, or 12th houses (though 8th and 12th can be spiritually strong)
- Conjoined with malefics (Saturn, Mars)
- In enemy's sign

## Ketu's Effects

Ketu creates:

- **Spiritual Growth**: Deep spiritual understanding and enlightenment
- **Detachment**: Freedom from material attachments
- **Mystical Abilities**: Psychic powers, intuition, occult knowledge
- **Isolation**: Preference for solitude and introspection
- **Transformation**: Deep inner transformation and change

## Dasha Period

- **Vimshottari Dasha**: 7 years
- **Effects**: Periods of spirituality, detachment, research, transformation, isolation, mystical experiences, past-life karma resolution

## Remedies

- Worship on Tuesdays or during Ketu Kaal
- Chanting Ketu mantras
- Wearing cat's eye (Lehsunia) or agate
- Fasting on Tuesdays
- Donating brown or multicolored items, or to spiritual causes
- Meditation and spiritual practices
