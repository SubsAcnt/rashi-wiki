# Jupiter (Guru) in Vedic Astrology

Jupiter, known as Guru or Brihaspati in Sanskrit, represents wisdom, knowledge, spirituality, and expansion. It is the greatest benefic and the teacher of the gods, bringing fortune and wisdom.

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: Guru, Brihaspati, Devaguru
- **Nature**: Benefic (Greatest Natural Benefic)
- **Element**: Ether/Space
- **Gender**: Male
- **Own Signs**: Sagittarius (Dhanu) and Pisces (Meena)
- **Exaltation**: Cancer (5 degrees)
- **Debilitation**: Capricorn (5 degrees)
- **Moolatrikona**: Sagittarius (0-10 degrees)

## Significations

Jupiter represents:

- **Wisdom and Knowledge**: Higher learning, philosophy, spirituality
- **Guru and Teachers**: Spiritual teachers, mentors, guides
- **Children**: Sons, progeny, fertility
- **Wealth and Fortune**: Financial prosperity, abundance
- **Religion and Dharma**: Righteousness, moral values, spirituality
- **Expansion**: Growth, prosperity, abundance
- **Liver**: Organ of metabolism and detoxification
- **Fat**: Body fat, obesity, metabolism
- **Hearing**: Ears, auditory functions
- **Charity**: Philanthropy, donations, generosity

## House Significations

Jupiter is particularly significant for:

- **2nd House**: Wealth, family, speech, knowledge
- **5th House**: Children, education, creativity, intelligence
- **9th House**: Dharma, father, higher learning, spirituality
- **11th House**: Gains, income, friends, aspirations
- **12th House**: Spirituality, foreign lands, expenses

## Strength Factors

Jupiter is strong when:

- Placed in Sagittarius or Pisces (own signs)
- In Cancer (exaltation)
- In angular houses (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th)
- In 9th house (dharma bhava)
- In 5th house (intelligence bhava)
- In own Nakshatra
- During daytime birth
- In Shukla Paksha (waxing moon)

## Weakness Factors

Jupiter is weak when:

- Placed in Capricorn (debilitation)
- In 6th, 8th, or 12th houses
- Combust (too close to Sun)
- During nighttime birth
- In enemy's sign

## Benefic Effects

Jupiter's benefic influence includes:

- **Financial Prosperity**: Wealth and abundance
- **Wisdom**: Knowledge and understanding
- **Children**: Progeny and fertility
- **Spirituality**: Religious and philosophical pursuits
- **Good Fortune**: Overall positive life experiences

## Dasha Period

- **Vimshottari Dasha**: 16 years
- **Effects**: Periods of wisdom, prosperity, children, education, spirituality, relationship with teachers, financial gains

## Remedies

- Worship on Thursdays
- Chanting Guru mantras
- Wearing yellow sapphire (Pukhraj)
- Fasting on Thursdays
- Donating yellow items, gold, or to teachers
- Reading scriptures and spiritual texts

## Related Concepts

- Guru Graha
- Jupiter's aspects
- Gajakesari Yoga (Jupiter-Moon conjunction)
- Hamsa Yoga (Jupiter in Kendra)
- Jupiter in different houses
- Spiritual growth and wisdom
