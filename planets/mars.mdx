# Mars (Mangal) in Vedic Astrology

Mars, known as Mangal or Kuja in Sanskrit, represents energy, courage, aggression, and action. It is the planet of warriors and signifies our ability to take initiative and overcome obstacles.

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: Mangal, Kuja, Angaraka
- **Nature**: Malefic
- **Element**: Fire
- **Gender**: Male
- **Own Signs**: Aries (Mesha) and Scorpio (Vrishchika)
- **Exaltation**: Capricorn (28 degrees)
- **Debilitation**: Cancer (28 degrees)
- **Moolatrikona**: Aries (0-12 degrees)

## Significations

Mars represents:

- **Energy and Courage**: Physical strength, bravery, determination
- **Brothers and Siblings**: Relationship with brothers, siblings
- **Land and Property**: Real estate, land ownership
- **Accidents**: Injuries, cuts, burns, surgical operations
- **Blood**: Blood-related issues, anemia, blood pressure
- **Muscles**: Physical strength, athletic ability
- **Weapons**: Tools, machinery, sharp objects
- **Fire**: Heat, inflammation, fever
- **Competition**: Sports, contests, rivalry

## House Significations

Mars is particularly significant for:

- **1st House**: Strong personality, leadership, courage
- **3rd House**: Brothers, courage, communication, short journeys
- **6th House**: Enemies, diseases, service, competition
- **10th House**: Career in military, engineering, surgery, sports
- **11th House**: Gains through courage and effort

## Strength Factors

Mars is strong when:

- Placed in Aries or Scorpio (own signs)
- In Capricorn (exaltation)
- In angular houses (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th)
- In 3rd house (parakrama bhava)
- In 10th house (karma bhava)
- During daytime birth
- In own Nakshatra

## Weakness Factors

Mars is weak when:

- Placed in Cancer (debilitation)
- In 6th, 8th, or 12th houses
- Combust (too close to Sun)
- During nighttime birth
- In enemy's sign

## Mangal Dosha (Mars Affliction)

Mars in certain houses creates Mangal Dosha:

- **Affected Houses**: 1st, 2nd, 4th, 7th, 8th, 12th
- **Effects**: Delayed marriage, marital discord
- **Cancellation**: If Mars is in own sign, exaltation, or aspected by benefics

## Dasha Period

- **Vimshottari Dasha**: 7 years
- **Effects**: Periods of energy, courage, conflicts, accidents, property matters, relationship with siblings

## Remedies

- Worship on Tuesdays
- Chanting Mangal mantras
- Wearing red coral (Moonga)
- Fasting on Tuesdays
- Donating red items, copper, or lentils
- Physical exercise and martial arts
