# Moon (Chandra) in Vedic Astrology

The Moon, known as Chandra in Sanskrit, represents the mind, emotions, mother, and public. It is the fastest-moving planet and reflects our emotional nature and mental state.

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: Chandra
- **Nature**: Benefic (when waxing), Malefic (when waning)
- **Element**: Water
- **Gender**: Female
- **Own Signs**: Cancer (Karka)
- **Exaltation**: Taurus (3 degrees)
- **Debilitation**: Scorpio (3 degrees)
- **Moolatrikona**: Taurus (3-27 degrees)

## Significations

The Moon represents:

- **Mind and Emotions**: Mental state, emotional stability, mood
- **Mother**: Relationship with mother, maternal figures
- **Public**: General public, masses, popularity
- **Fluids**: Water, blood, milk, all liquids
- **Travel**: Short journeys, movement
- **Fertility**: Conception, pregnancy, childbirth
- **Memory**: Ability to remember and retain information
- **Intuition**: Psychic abilities, sixth sense

## House Significations

The Moon is particularly significant for:

- **1st House**: Emotional personality, public image
- **4th House**: Mother, home, property, comfort
- **7th House**: Spouse, partnerships, marriage
- **10th House**: Public recognition, career in public service
- **11th House**: Gains, income, friends

## Strength Factors

The Moon is strong when:

- Placed in Cancer (own sign)
- In Taurus (exaltation)
- Waxing phase (Shukla Paksha)
- In angular houses (1st, 4th, 7th, 10th)
- In 4th house (sukha bhava)
- During nighttime birth
- Full Moon (Purnima)

## Weakness Factors

The Moon is weak when:

- Placed in Scorpio (debilitation)
- Waning phase (Krishna Paksha)
- In 6th, 8th, or 12th houses
- New Moon (Amavasya)
- During daytime birth
- Combust or too close to Sun

## Nakshatra Rulership

The Moon rules all 27 Nakshatras, spending approximately 1 day in each:

- **Ashwini to Revati**: Complete cycle of 27.3 days
- Each Nakshatra has specific characteristics and deities
- Moon's position in Nakshatra determines mental tendencies

## Dasha Period

- **Vimshottari Dasha**: 10 years
- **Effects**: Periods of emotional changes, relationship with mother, public interactions, travel, fertility matters

## Remedies

- Worship on Mondays
- Chanting Chandra mantras
- Wearing pearl (Moti)
- Fasting on Mondays
- Donating rice, white items, or milk
- Moon meditation and water therapy

## Related Concepts

- Chandra Graha
- Moon phases (Tithi)
- Nakshatras
- Moon's aspects
- Lunar eclipses
- Sun-Moon relationship (Yoga)
