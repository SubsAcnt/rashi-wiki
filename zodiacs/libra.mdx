# Libra (Tula) - The Scales

Libra is the seventh sign of the zodiac, representing balance, relationships, and justice. It is an air sign ruled by Venus, the planet of beauty, harmony, and partnerships.

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: Tula
- **Element**: Air (Vayu)
- **Quality**: Movable (Chara)
- **Gender**: Male (Pumsa)
- **Ruling Planet**: Venus
- **Exalted Planet**: Saturn
- **Debilitated Planet**: Sun
- **Body Part**: Lower back and kidneys

## Characteristics

### Personality Traits

- **Balanced**: Seek harmony and equilibrium
- **Diplomatic**: Excellent at mediation and compromise
- **Social**: Love relationships and partnerships
- **Aesthetic**: Appreciation for beauty and art
- **Indecisive**: Difficulty making choices
- **Peace-loving**: Avoid conflict and confrontation

### Positive Qualities

- Natural sense of balance and justice
- Excellent diplomatic skills
- Appreciation for beauty and aesthetics
- Strong relationship abilities
- Fair and just nature
- Charming and sociable

### Challenges

- Indecisiveness and hesitation
- Tendency to avoid conflict
- May be overly dependent on others
- Can be superficial at times
- Difficulty being alone
- May compromise too much

## Career and Professions

Libra natives excel in:

- Law and judiciary
- Mediation and arbitration
- Fashion and design
- Arts and entertainment
- Public relations
- Counseling and therapy
- Interior design
- Marriage counseling
- Beauty and cosmetics industry

## Health and Body Parts

Libra rules the lower back and kidneys, so natives should pay attention to:

- Kidney problems
- Lower back pain
- Urinary tract issues
- Skin problems
- Hormonal imbalances
- Stress-related conditions
- Balance and coordination

## Relationships and Compatibility

### Best Matches

- Gemini (air sign, intellectual connection)
- Aquarius (air sign, shared values)
- Leo (creative partnership)
- Sagittarius (adventurous spirit)

### Challenges

- May find Cancer too emotional
- Can conflict with Aries (direct vs. diplomatic)
- May struggle with Capricorn's practicality

## Spiritual and Philosophical Aspects

Libra represents:

- The importance of balance and harmony
- The value of relationships and partnerships
- Justice and fairness
- The search for beauty and aesthetics
- The balance between self and others
- The path of diplomacy and peace

## Famous Libra Personalities

Many artists, lawyers, and diplomats are born under Libra, reflecting the sign's emphasis on balance, justice, and aesthetics.

## Remedies and Strengthening

- Worship Goddess Lakshmi (Venus deity)
- Wear diamond or white sapphire (Venus gemstones)
- Chant Venus mantras
- Practice decision-making
- Engage in artistic activities
- Maintain balance in life
