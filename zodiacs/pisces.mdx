# Pisces (Meena) - The Fish

Pisces is the twelfth and final sign of the zodiac, representing spirituality, compassion, and transcendence. It is a water sign ruled by Jupiter, the planet of wisdom, expansion, and spirituality.

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: Meena
- **Element**: Water (Jala)
- **Quality**: Dual (Dwiswabhava)
- **Gender**: Female (Stree)
- **Ruling Planet**: Jupiter
- **Exalted Planet**: Venus
- **Debilitated Planet**: Mercury
- **Body Part**: Feet

## Characteristics

### Personality Traits

- **Compassionate**: Deep empathy and understanding
- **Intuitive**: Strong psychic and spiritual abilities
- **Artistic**: Creative and imaginative nature
- **Sensitive**: Highly emotional and feeling
- **Dreamy**: Idealistic and visionary
- **Selfless**: Giving and sacrificing nature

### Positive Qualities

- Deep compassion and empathy
- Strong intuition and psychic abilities
- Artistic and creative talents
- Spiritual and philosophical nature
- Adaptable and flexible
- Selfless service to others

### Challenges

- Can be overly sensitive
- Tendency to escape reality
- May be too idealistic
- Difficulty setting boundaries
- Can be easily influenced
- May lack practicality

## Career and Professions

Pisces natives excel in:

- Arts and creative fields
- Music and performing arts
- Spirituality and religion
- Psychology and counseling
- Healing and alternative medicine
- Photography and film
- Charity and social work
- Marine and water-related fields
- Mystical and esoteric studies

## Health and Body Parts

Pisces rules the feet, so natives should pay attention to:

- Foot problems
- Lymphatic system issues
- Sleep disorders
- Addiction and escapism
- Mental and emotional health
- Immune system problems
- Water retention

## Relationships and Compatibility

### Best Matches

- Cancer (water sign, emotional understanding)
- Scorpio (water sign, deep connection)
- Taurus (emotional security)
- Capricorn (practical support)

### Challenges

- May find Gemini too intellectual
- Can conflict with Virgo (dreamy vs. practical)
- May struggle with Sagittarius's directness

## Spiritual and Philosophical Aspects

Pisces represents:

- The path of spirituality and transcendence
- The importance of compassion and service
- The connection to the divine and universal
- The dissolution of ego and boundaries
- The balance between material and spiritual
- The completion of the zodiac cycle

## Famous Pisces Personalities

Many artists, musicians, and spiritual leaders are born under Pisces, reflecting the sign's creative and compassionate nature.

## Remedies and Strengthening

- Worship Lord Vishnu (Jupiter deity)
- Wear yellow sapphire (Jupiter gemstone)
- Chant Jupiter mantras
- Practice grounding and reality checks
- Engage in spiritual practices
- Maintain boundaries and self-care
