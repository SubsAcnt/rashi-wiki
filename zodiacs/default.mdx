# Zodiac Signs in Vedic Astrology

The 12 zodiac signs, known as Rashis in Vedic astrology, form the foundation of the birth chart. Each sign has unique characteristics, ruling planets, and significations that influence personality, behavior, and life events.

## The 12 Rashis

The zodiac is divided into 12 equal signs of 30 degrees each:

1. **[Aries (Mesha)](/wiki/zodiacs/aries)** - The Ram
2. **[Taurus (Vrishabha)](/wiki/zodiacs/taurus)** - The Bull
3. **[Gemini (Mithuna)](/wiki/zodiacs/gemini)** - The Twins
4. **[Cancer (Karka)](/wiki/zodiacs/cancer)** - The Crab
5. **[Leo (Simha)](/wiki/zodiacs/leo)** - The Lion
6. **[Virgo (Kanya)](/wiki/zodiacs/virgo)** - The Virgin
7. **[Libra (Tula)](/wiki/zodiacs/libra)** - The Scales
8. **[Scorpio (Vrishchika)](/wiki/zodiacs/scorpio)** - The Scorpion
9. **[Sagittarius (Dhanu)](/wiki/zodiacs/sagittarius)** - The Archer
10. **[Capricorn (Makara)](/wiki/zodiacs/capricorn)** - The Sea-Goat
11. **[Aquarius (Kumbha)](/wiki/zodiacs/aquarius)** - The Water-Bearer
12. **[Pisces (Meena)](/wiki/zodiacs/pisces)** - The Fish

## Classification of Signs

### By Element (Tattva)

- **Fire Signs (Agni)**: Aries, Leo, Sagittarius - Active, energetic, passionate
- **Earth Signs (Prithvi)**: Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn - Practical, stable, materialistic
- **Air Signs (Vayu)**: Gemini, Libra, Aquarius - Intellectual, communicative, social
- **Water Signs (Jala)**: Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces - Emotional, intuitive, sensitive

### By Quality (Guna)

- **Movable Signs (Chara)**: Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn - Dynamic, initiating
- **Fixed Signs (Sthira)**: Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius - Stable, determined
- **Dual Signs (Dwiswabhava)**: Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces - Adaptable, versatile

### By Gender

- **Male Signs (Pumsa)**: Aries, Gemini, Leo, Libra, Sagittarius, Aquarius
- **Female Signs (Stree)**: Taurus, Cancer, Virgo, Scorpio, Capricorn, Pisces

## Ruling Planets

Each sign is ruled by a specific planet:

- **Aries & Scorpio**: Mars
- **Taurus & Libra**: Venus
- **Gemini & Virgo**: Mercury
- **Cancer**: Moon
- **Leo**: Sun
- **Sagittarius & Pisces**: Jupiter
- **Capricorn & Aquarius**: Saturn

## Sign Characteristics

Each sign represents:

- **Specific body parts** (medical astrology)
- **Professions and careers**
- **Personality traits**
- **Life areas and experiences**
- **Compatibility with other signs**

## Importance in Chart Analysis

The zodiac signs are crucial for:

- Understanding personality and behavior
- Determining planetary strength (exaltation, debilitation)
- Analyzing house significations
- Predicting life events and timing
- Assessing compatibility and relationships
