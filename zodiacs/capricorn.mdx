# Capricorn (Makara) - The Sea-Goat

Capricorn is the tenth sign of the zodiac, representing ambition, discipline, and achievement. It is an earth sign ruled by Saturn, the planet of discipline, karma, and time.

## Basic Information

- **Sanskrit Name**: Makara
- **Element**: Earth (Prithvi)
- **Quality**: Movable (Chara)
- **Gender**: Female (Stree)
- **Ruling Planet**: Saturn
- **Exalted Planet**: Mars
- **Debilitated Planet**: Jupiter
- **Body Part**: Knees and bones

## Characteristics

### Personality Traits

- **Ambitious**: Strong drive for success and achievement
- **Disciplined**: Self-control and determination
- **Practical**: Realistic and goal-oriented
- **Responsible**: Reliable and dependable
- **Patient**: Long-term thinking and planning
- **Reserved**: Can be serious and cautious

### Positive Qualities

- Strong work ethic and discipline
- Excellent organizational skills
- Long-term planning abilities
- Reliability and responsibility
- Natural leadership in business
- Ability to overcome obstacles

### Challenges

- Can be overly serious or pessimistic
- Tendency to be workaholic
- May be too cautious or fearful
- Difficulty expressing emotions
- Can be rigid or inflexible
- May focus too much on material success

## Career and Professions

Capricorn natives excel in:

- Business and management
- Government and administration
- Engineering and construction
- Real estate and property
- Mining and geology
- Architecture and planning
- Finance and banking
- Law and judiciary
- Long-term strategic planning

## Health and Body Parts

Capricorn rules the knees and bones, so natives should pay attention to:

- Bone-related problems
- Joint and knee issues
- Teeth and dental problems
- Skin conditions
- Depression and mental health
- Chronic conditions
- Aging-related issues

## Relationships and Compatibility

### Best Matches

- Taurus (earth sign, practical partnership)
- Virgo (earth sign, shared values)
- Scorpio (deep understanding)
- Pisces (emotional support)

### Challenges

- May find Aries too impulsive
- Can conflict with Cancer (practical vs. emotional)
- May struggle with Libra's need for balance

## Spiritual and Philosophical Aspects

Capricorn represents:

- The importance of discipline and hard work
- The value of patience and persistence
- The path of karma and responsibility
- The balance between material and spiritual
- The lessons of time and aging
- The achievement through effort

## Famous Capricorn Personalities

Many business leaders, administrators, and achievers are born under Capricorn, reflecting the sign's ambition and discipline.

## Remedies and Strengthening

- Worship Lord Shani (Saturn deity)
- Wear blue sapphire (Saturn gemstone)
- Chant Saturn mantras
- Practice patience and discipline
- Engage in service and charity
- Maintain work-life balance
